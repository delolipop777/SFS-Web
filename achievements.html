Our Achievements

The Sustainable Future Society (SFS) is proud to be recognized as an Eco-School. This prestigious title reflects our commitment to sustainability and environmental awareness.

Thanks to the efforts of our dedicated members, the Presidential School in Namangan has been awarded the Green Flag for its outstanding environmental initiatives. This achievement highlights our success in implementing the Eco-Schools program and fostering a culture of sustainability within our community.

We continue to strive for excellence in promoting ecological awareness and sustainable practices, making a positive impact on our environment and inspiring others to join our mission.
