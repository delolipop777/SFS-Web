About Sustainable Future Society

The Sustainable Future Society (SFS) is an environmental volunteer club run by the active students of the Presidential School in Namangan. The club's main goal is to protect the environment, raise ecological awareness, and promote a sustainable lifestyle.

Under the leadership of Abrorjon Sadriddionov, the coordinator of the Eco-Schools Namangan program, SFS has achieved remarkable milestones, including earning the prestigious Green Flag for its environmental initiatives.
