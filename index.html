About SFS

The Sustainable Future Society (SFS) is led by active students of the Presidential School in Namangan. Our mission is to protect the environment, raise ecological awareness, and promote sustainable living.

Under the guidance of Abrorjon Sadriddionov, SFS has achieved significant milestones, including earning the Green Flag for its environmental initiatives.

Discover more about our 7-step Eco-Schools program, our dedicated team, and our achievements in sustainability.
